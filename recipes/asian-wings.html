Asian chicken wings

Fried chicken wings with sweet and sour sauce, sesame seeds and chives

This world-renowned snack is quick and easy to prepare. Try this recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 kg of chicken wings
  • Black pepper, q.s.
  • 1 teaspoon rated fresh ginger
  • 50 g of potato starch
  • Oil, q.b.
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 3 chili peppers
  • 125 ml of honey
  • 60 ml of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon white vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • Sesame seeds, q.s.
  • Chive, q.s.

Steps

  1. Cut the chicken wings in half and season them with black pepper and ginger.
  2. Pass them through the potato starch, pressing them well so that they are well breaded.
  3. Fry them in oil for 12 minutes, turning them occasionally.
  4. Remove them and conserve them.
  5. Prepare the sauce: in a frying pan, heat 2 tablespoons of oil together with the chopped garlic cloves and the seeded and chopped chili peppers and cook for 30 seconds.
  6. Add honey, soy sauce, vinegar and mustard.
  7. Let it bubble for a few minutes, stirring constantly until it thickens.
  8. Add sugar, stirring until melted, then remove from heat.
  9. Fry the wings a second time for 15 minutes and then wrap them in the reheated sauce.
  10. Sprinkle them with sesame seeds and chopped chives and serve.
