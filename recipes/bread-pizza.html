Bread with pizza filling

Bread with filling of mozzarella cheese, tomato pulp and sliced chorizo

Savor the delicious Bread with Pizza Stuffing - a quick and delicious option to satisfy your pizza craving at any time.

Ingredients

  • Salt, q.s.
  • Black pepper, q.s.
  • Olive oil, q.s.
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 200g of sliced chorizo
  • 250ml of fried tomato pulp
  • 1 large Rio Maior bread
  • 250g sliced mozzarella cheese
  • Oregano, to taste

Steps

  1. In a skillet put a thread of olive oil and sauté the onion until golden.
  2. Add the sliced chorizo until sautéed, and then add the fried tomato pulp. Let it cook for 5 minutes.
  3. Open a lid on the Rio Maior bread. Reserve the cap. Remove all the core, leaving the bread hollow.
  4. Place a portion of chorizo with tomato sauce on the bread. Intersperse layers of mozzarella cheese, chorizo with tomato sauce and oregano. Put the top part of the bread back on.
  5. Wrap the bread in aluminum foil and bake at 180ºC for 20 minutes or until the cheese is completely melted.
  6. Serve it hot.
