Chicken and Mushroom Stroganoff

Chicken and Mushroom Stroganoff with a side of white rice and fries

Delight yourself with a tasty chicken and mushroom stroganoff. An irresistible combination of flavors in an incredible dish.

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken breast
  • Salt, q.s.
  • Black pepper, q.s.
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 100g mushrooms
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoon dijon mustard
  • 125g light cream
  • 30g tomato pulp
  • Chopped chives, q.s.
  • Long grain rice, to serve
  • Thin straw potatoes, to serve

Steps

  1. Start by cutting the chicken into thin strips and season them with salt and pepper to taste
  2. In a large frying pan with a drizzle of olive oil, place the onion and let it cook, stirring until the onion is translucent.
  3. Add the chicken strips and let it brown for a few minutes over low heat.
  4. Add previously sliced mushrooms and mix well. Add soy sauce, tomato paste, dijon mustard and cream. Adjust seasonings and cook for 5 minutes.
  5. Serve with long rice and thin straw potatoes and sprinkle chopped chives on top.
